BRUTALISM is a studio focused on crafting precise generative systems and exploring their practical applications in interactive software, industrial design, and architecture.

This website is both a content hub and a space for contemplating new ideas and future work. The Log section contains shorter, more frequent updates, while the Process section holds longer-form articles explaining the thinking and the methods used while working on various projects.
